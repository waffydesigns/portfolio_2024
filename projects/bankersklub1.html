The Business Challenge

In today's industry corporates are constantly seeking seasoned financial experts to navigate challenges and seize growth opportunities. Retired bankers, with their unmatched experience and deep industry knowledge, are a perfect fit to meet these demands. Yet, there was a glaring void—no platform existed to bring these two worlds together.

Key challenges

addressed by Bankersklub
• Limited Access to Expert Bankers:
Without a dedicated space, corporates struggled to find and engage retired bankers, who often lacked a digital presence. Many of these professionals are not highly tech-savvy, so the need for an intuitive, banking-specific platform was clear. BankersKlub was designed to simplify access and encourage participation by prioritizing ease of use and familiar workflows.
• No Existing Solution:
Before BankersKlub, there was no specialized platform tailored for this niche. BankersKlub emerged as a transformative solution, empowering retired bankers to extend their impact and enabling corporates to harness their expertise in a structured and efficient way.
• A Fragmented Connection Process:
Corporates and retired bankers relied on ad hoc and inefficient methods to connect, which often led to missed opportunities and disjointed collaborations.
By addressing these challenges, BankersKlub set a new standard for how corporates and retired bankers connect and collaborate.

Crafting the Experience Strategy

BankersKlub’s design approach was anchored in a deep understanding of the distinct user groups—retired banking professionals and corporate clients. Extensive research helped identify why users often struggled on existing platforms and what was needed to create a more aligned, goal-oriented experience.
‍

Key insights from the research showed:

Corporate clients required a straightforward, intuitive way to search for specialized advisors and establish relevant connections efficiently.

Retired bankers needed a platform that allowed them to easily showcase their expertise, making it accessible and attractive to potential corporate clients.

Both groups benefited from clear guidance through their journey, ensuring they could achieve their objectives without friction.


These insights informed BankersKlub’s user experience strategy, which focused on simplifying workflows, increasing visibility for advisors, and building in proactive engagement features to enhance usability, retention, and overall satisfaction.
